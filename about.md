## What I do

Possible is a legacy software recovery and modernization business based in Australia.

I help organisations recover, understand, and modernize software when the normal options aren't available — when source code is missing, when the vendor has disappeared, when the developer retired, or when the platform is no longer supported.

I've built capability to analyse legacy Windows applications and proprietary data formats directly from what exists: running software, executable files, database files. I produce documentation, data extracts, and actionable plans — without requiring source code.

## What I don't do

I don't sell technology. I sell outcomes.

You don't need to understand how I do what I do. You need your data out of a dead file format, or a clear picture of what your undocumented system actually does, or a realistic plan for replacing it. That's what I deliver.

## Who I work with

I work with small and medium businesses, IT managers, operations teams, and finance teams who are dealing with a legacy software problem that nobody else will take seriously.

The businesses I help typically share a common experience: they've contacted a software developer or IT provider, been told "that's too complicated" or "you should have migrated years ago," and ended up with no path forward. I specialise in exactly those situations.
